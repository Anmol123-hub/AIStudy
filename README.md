AIStudy – Offline AI Study Buddy
📚 Overview

AIStudy is an offline-first AI-powered study companion designed for students in India and other emerging markets where:

Internet connectivity is unreliable
Students use low-end Android phones
English is not always the primary language
Personalized tutoring is expensive or unavailable

AIStudy uses lightweight on-device AI models like Google Gemma to provide a private, fast, and affordable learning experience — fully offline.

🚀 Vision

Make high-quality AI education accessible to every student, even without internet.

AIStudy transforms an Android phone into:

A personal tutor
A doubt solver
A quiz generator
A multilingual explainer
A PDF summarizer
A voice-based learning assistant
